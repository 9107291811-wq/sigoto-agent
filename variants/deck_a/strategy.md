# Deck A Strategy

Current main strategy:

- Build Okidogi and Lillie's Clefairy ex as primary attackers.
- Keep Solrock and Lunatone as early draw support, but do not overvalue them once attackers matter.
- Use Barbaracle to accelerate Basic Fighting Energy when it completes an attacker or enables a retreat into a prepared attacker.
- Prefer Legacy Energy before Prism Energy when either special Energy can satisfy the same attacker requirement.
- Use Boss's Orders mainly for EX knockouts, high-energy threats, and Clefairy weakness targets such as Mega Lucario ex or Dragapult ex.
